Rules

Player must first collect the blue and orange gems, then the key. Lastly the star must be collected to complete each level and score points.

  • Use arrow keys to move player left, right, up and down
  • Collect a blue gem to score 50 points
  • Collect an orange gem to score 100 points
  • Collect the key to score 200 points
  • You must avoid the bugs to stay alive
  • Enjoy the game!
